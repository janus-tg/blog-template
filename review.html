Reviews

The Sign of Four

Sir Arthur Conan Doyle

Everything seems quiet in London. Sherlock Holmes is bored and somewhat disappointed by this lack of crime on the streets. Watson finds Holmes in a haze induced by his famous 7% cocaine solution. Holmes' spirit is only lifted when a young woman, Mary Morstan, comes with an unusual case. The story revolves around an Indian treasure. Some convicts of the despicable Andaman Jail shares the secret of the treasure with 2 English officers. The convicts and the officers have a deal but no one is in a mood to honor it. A hunt begins which even made Holmes work for the solution.

Origin

Dan Brown

Robert Langdon has been invited by his student and now entrepeneur Edmund Kirsch to a grand revealing show where he plans to tell the world about how life came on Earth- the story of origin. But, he is murdered before he can reveal this. Now, it is upto Langdon and the future queen of Spain- Ambra Vidal to uncover the identity of the killer and reveal Kirsch's findings to the world.

Hamlet

William Shakespeare

This famous story revolves around Hamlet- the Prince of Denmark. Hamlet's father, the king has died under mysterious circumstances. Hamlet's mother has married the late King's brother who has now become the king. Hamlet is visited by his father's ghost who tells him that his own brother murdered him. Now, it is upto Hamlet to avenge his father's death.

The Godfather

Mario Puzo

This story chroncicles the life of the Corleone mafia family in NYC. Don Vito Corleone is widely respected. When a deal goes bad and Vito is almost murdered. Things turn worse when Sonny, Vito's heir, is brutally murdered. Now, it's upto the ailing Vito and his youngest son Michael to navigate their family out of these perilous waters.
